BPMCE Gaming Community

Free Fire Tournament Schedule

Round 1 – Battle Royale (Ranked Qualifiers)

🕒 Time: 12:00 PM – 1:00 PM

16 Teams will play in 2 rooms:

  • Room 1 → 8 Teams
  • Room 2 → 8 Teams

Top 5 Teams from each room will qualify.

👉 A total of 10 Teams advance.

Round 2 – Battle Royale (Ranked Quarterfinals)

🕒 Time: 1:30 PM – 2:15 PM

The 10 qualified teams battle together in one BR match.

Top 4 Teams (based on kills + placement) advance to the Semi-Finals.

Round 3 – Clash Squad (Ranked Semi-Finals)

🕒 Time: 2:45 PM – 3:30 PM

The Top 4 Teams face off in CS mode.

Top 2 Teams qualify for the Grand Final.

Round 4 – Clash Squad (Ranked Grand Final)

🕒 Time: 4:00 PM – 5:00 PM

The Top 2 Teams play a Best of 3 Clash Squad Final.

The winner will be crowned 🏆 BPMCE Free Fire Champion 2025 🎉🔥

📌 Important Notes

  • Room ID & Password will be shared 15 mins before match time.
  • Teams must be ready 10 mins before start.
  • Hacks/cheats = 🚫 instant disqualification.
  • BR Scoring: Kills + Placement Points
  • CS Scoring: Round Wins

🔥 Format Recap:

16 Teams → Top 10 → Top 4 → Top 2 → 🏆 1 Champion
